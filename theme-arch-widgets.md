# Arch Theme Widgets Guide

This document provides a detailed overview of all widgets available in the **Arch** theme. Each widget is designed to be flexible, responsive, and easy to customize through the Widgetizer editor.

---

## Banners & Heroes

High-impact sections typically used at the top of pages to grab attention.

### Banner

A versatile hero section for page introductions.

- **Key Features**: Supports background images/colors, overlay adjustments, and flexible blocks for headings, text, and button groups.
- **Best For**: Homepages, landing pages, and section headers.

### Slideshow

An interactive carousel for cycling through multiple banners.

- **Key Features**: Autoplay with adjustable speed, full-width support, and independent slide styling (text color, background, buttons).
- **Best For**: Showcasing multiple featured items or promotions.

---

## Content & Media

Widgets for displaying rich media and descriptive text content.

### Image + Text

A balanced layout pairing an image with text.

- **Key Features**: Alternating image positions (left/right), multiple heading sizes, feature lists, and dual call-to-action buttons.
- **Best For**: Explaining features, company history, or services.

### Image Callout

A focused media block with optional features list.

- **Key Features**: Similar to Image + Text but optimized for smaller "callout" sections with a structured feature list block.
- **Best For**: Highlighting specific benefits or "Why Choose Us" sections.

### Image Hotspots

An interactive image with clickable tooltips.

- **Key Features**: Custom-positioned markers (%) with titles, descriptions, and optional buttons.
- **Best For**: Product feature tours or complex diagrams.

### Image Tabs

Tabbed navigation where each tab shows a different image and description.

- **Key Features**: Horizontal tab layout with synchronized image/text swapping.
- **Best For**: Product features or multi-step processes with visual aids.

### Video

A self-hosted video player for MP4 files.

- **Key Features**: Autoplay, loop, muted, and aspect ratio controls (16:9, 4:3, etc.).
- **Best For**: High-quality product demos or background videos.

### YouTube/Vimeo

Embed external video content easily.

- **Key Features**: Simply paste a URL; supports responsive aspect ratios.
- **Best For**: Third-party hosted videos to save on bandwidth.

### Podcast Player

A dedicated player for audio episodes.

- **Key Features**: Simple audio controls (volume, speed), artwork display, and episode metadata (date, duration).
- **Best For**: Shows, interviews, or audio guides.

### Gallery

A standard grid of images with captions.

- **Key Features**: Simple block-based image additions with individual captions.
- **Best For**: Portfolios or event photos.

### Masonry Gallery

A dynamic "Pinterest-style" grid for images of varying heights.

- **Key Features**: Automatic layout that fills gaps, categories for each item.
- **Best For**: Creative portfolios and visual mood boards.

### Embed Widget

A container for custom HTML, IFrames, or scripts.

- **Key Features**: Adjustable width and alignment for external integrations.
- **Best For**: Custom forms, calendars, or interactive widgets from other platforms.

## Layout & Grids

Widgets for organizing information into structured, easy-to-read formats.

### Card Grid

A responsive grid of cards with icons or images.

- **Key Features**: Supports icons, images, subtitles, and individual call-to-action buttons.
- **Best For**: Services overview, feature lists, or team highlights.

### Bento Grid

A modern, asymmetrical layout with varying tile sizes.

- **Key Features**: Adjustable column and row spans for each "tile" to create a dashboard look.
- **Best For**: Product highlights or visually complex feature summaries.

### Alternating Rows

A zigzagging list of feature sections.

- **Key Features**: Automatically flips image/text orientation for each row.
- **Best For**: Long-form landing pages explaining complex products.

### Featured Cards

Specialized cards for highlighting items with tags and prices.

- **Key Features**: Supports "Chef's Pick" or "New" tags, original vs. discounted prices, and list/card toggle.
- **Best For**: Today's specials, featured products, or limited offers.

### Numbered Cards

An ordered list of steps or process items.

- **Key Features**: Automatically increments numbers for each block.
- **Best For**: "How it Works" or "Our Process" sections.

### Profile Grid

A grid for team members or staff profiles.

- **Key Features**: Detailed fields for role, specialty, bio, and multiple social media links.
- **Best For**: "Meet the Team" or "About Us" pages.

### Accordion

Expandable content sections for dense information.

- **Key Features**: Style toggle (separated vs. connected), "allow multiple open" setting.
- **Best For**: FAQs and technical specifications.

### Content Switcher

Toggle between different content views (e.g., Monthly vs. Yearly).

- **Key Features**: Supports up to 3 options (Tabs) or 2 (Toggle), with custom grid layouts for each state.
- **Best For**: Pricing tiers or feature comparisons.

### Timeline

Show a sequence of events or a project roadmap.

- **Key Features**: Vertical, horizontal, or centered layouts; supports date/phase and duration fields.
- **Best For**: Company history or product roadmaps.

### Event List

A vertical list of upcoming happenings.

- **Key Features**: Large date indicators (Day/Month), location fields, and registration buttons.
- **Best For**: Webinars, workshops, or conferences.

### Schedule Table

A structured table for time-based information.

- **Key Features**: Set opening hours or class schedules with "Closed" indicators.
- **Best For**: Business hours, gym schedules, or restaurant timing.

## Business & Marketing

Sales-focused widgets for conversion and social proof.

### Pricing

Standard pricing tiers for products or services.

- **Key Features**: "Featured plan" highlight, feature list (one per line), and period settings (e.g., /month).
- **Best For**: SaaS products and subscription services.

### Comparison Table

A checklist-style table for comparing plans.

- **Key Features**: Highlighted columns, badges (e.g., "Popular"), and check/cross (✓/✗) indicators.
- **Best For**: Helping users choose between complex pricing tiers.

### Comparison Slider

An interactive "Before and After" comparison tool.

- **Key Features**: Horizontal or vertical slider, custom labels, and initial position control.
- **Best For**: Photo editing, renovations, or weight loss results.

### Logo Cloud

A grid of partner or client logos.

- **Key Features**: Simple logo blocks with optional company names and links.
- **Best For**: Trust signals and social proof.

### Testimonials

Customer reviews and feedback.

- **Key Features**: Star ratings (3-5 stars), avatars, and title/company fields.
- **Best For**: Building credibility and converting visitors.

### Key Figures

Big numbers and statistics.

- **Key Features**: Optional number animation, custom suffixes (+, %, /7), and card/bar layouts.
- **Best For**: Impact reports or marketing stats.

### Priced List

A catalog for menus or services with multiple tags.

- **Key Features**: Extensive badges for dietary requirements (Vegetarian, Vegan, GF, Nut-Free, Spicy, Halal, etc.).
- **Best For**: Restaurant menus and detailed service catalogs.

---

## Interactive & Forms

Engagement points for lead generation and user interaction.

### Contact Form

A flexible form for receiving messages.

- **Key Features**: Customizable fields (Email, Tel, Select, Checkbox), sidebar with contact info, and custom submission URL.
- **Best For**: General inquiries and lead capture.

### Newsletter Signup

A simple email subscription box.

- **Key Features**: Success messages and customizable placeholders.
- **Best For**: Growing mailing lists and customer retention.

### Map

Responsive Google Maps integration.

- **Key Features**: IFrame-based embed with adjustable height and address display.
- **Best For**: Showing physical store locations or event venues.

### Countdown Timer

A live timer for deadlines or launches.

- **Key Features**: Target date setting, expired message, and modern "card" or "minimal" styles.
- **Best For**: Launch events, sales deadlines, or upcoming features.

---

## Global Components

Universal elements consistently used across all pages.

### Header

The primary navigation at the top of the site.

- **Key Features**: Logo image/text toggle, navigation menu selector, and primary CTA button.

### Footer

The base of the site containing copyright and useful links.
